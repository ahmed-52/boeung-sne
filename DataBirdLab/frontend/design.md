**Theme:** A calming, dark-mode aesthetic inspired by nature. High contrast is used for data legibility, while the background remains muted to reduce eye strain during monitoring.

* **Color Palette:**
    * **Background:** Deep Olive / Sage Green (Matte finish).
    * **Card Surface:** Lighter, translucent Sage Green (Glassmorphism effect).
    * **Text (Primary):** Pure White (`#FFFFFF`) for headers and key data.
    * **Text (Secondary):** Light Grey / Silver for subtitles and metadata.
    * **Accents:** Soft Yellow (for weather/sun icons) or Cyan (for active status indicators).
* **Typography:**
    * **Font Family:** Modern Sans-Serif (e.g., Gotham, Montserrat, or Roboto).
    * **Style:**
        * *Page Header:* Large, friendly sentence case.
        * *Card Headers:* Small, uppercase, tracked-out (wide letter spacing).
        * *Data Values:* Large, thin weight.
